Beginner Information Pack

Welcome to the dojo. This short guide helps new students prepare for their first month of training.

What to Bring

Comfortable sports clothes, water bottle, and a respectful attitude. A karate gi can be arranged after the first trial period.

Dojo Etiquette

Arrive a few minutes early, bow when entering and leaving the training area, listen carefully, and train safely with partners.

First Month

New students focus on basic stances, blocks, punches, movement, and confidence in the class routine.
